Our Story

Born in a Kitchen,
Built on Trust

Ayini Home Products started with a simple question: "Why can't we buy the same quality masalas and oils our grandmothers used to make?"

What began as small batches for family and friends in PeriyanaickenPalayam, Coimbatore slowly grew into something bigger — a brand built entirely on word of mouth and the trust of our customers.

Every product we make is prepared in small batches at home, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. No factories. No preservatives. No shortcuts.
